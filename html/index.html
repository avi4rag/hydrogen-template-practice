MARVAL STUDIOS

MARVAL STUDIOS is an American film and television production company that is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios, a division of The Walt Disney Company. It is known for producing films and television series based on characters from Marvel Comics.

THE AVENGERS:

The first Avengers team of superheroes in the Marvel Comics universe. The team was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby and first appeared in The Avengers #1 in 1963. The original lineup of the team included Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk, Ant-Man, and the Wasp.Released on 1st September 1963.

THE FIRST MARVAL STUDIOS MOVIE

The first Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) movie is Iron Man, released in 2008, which kicked off the shared universe of interconnected superhero films and shows. If you're referring to the first-ever Marvel-based movie, regardless of the MCU, it would be Howard the Duck (1986), though it was not a major success.
